Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a test drive?

You can schedule a test drive by calling our sales team at (800) TES-LAIN, using our online scheduling tool, or visiting any of our showrooms. Test drives typically last 30 minutes and require a valid driver's license.

What is your vehicle delivery process?

Once your vehicle is ready, we'll contact you to schedule delivery. We offer both in-dealership pickup and home delivery within 100 miles. Our delivery specialist will walk you through all vehicle features, complete paperwork, and answer any questions.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer competitive financing options through our network of lenders. You can apply online for pre-approval or work with our financing specialists in person. We also accept Tesla financing and third-party financing arrangements.

How often does my Tesla need maintenance?

Tesla vehicles require significantly less maintenance than traditional cars. We recommend annual check-ups for optimal performance. Key maintenance includes tire rotation every 10,000-12,000 miles, brake fluid check every 2 years, and cabin air filter replacement every 2 years.

Can I trade in my current vehicle?

Absolutely! We accept trade-ins for all makes and models. You can get an instant online estimate or bring your vehicle to any Tesla Ink location for a professional appraisal. Trade-in value can be applied directly to your new Tesla purchase.
